Allow your customers to subscribe to your newsletter during checkout. Add-on for Nifty Newsletters and WooCommerce.

== Description ==

Allow your customers to subscribe to your newsletter during checkout.

> **Please note**
> This is an add-on for [Nifty Newsletters](https://wordpress.org/plugins/sola-newsletters/), [Nifty Newsletters Pro](http://solaplugins.com/plugins/sola-newsletters/) and [WooCommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce/)

Once installed, this add-on will enable a new set of options to Nifty Newsletters, as well as a new checkbox that will be added to your checkout page to allow your customers to subscribe to your newsletters.

== Installation ==

1. Please ensure you first have Nifty Newsletters and WooCommerce installed and activated
2. Activate Nifty Newsletters - WooCommerce Subscriptions
3. Settings are available in the Newsletters -> Settings page
4. A checkbox will be added to to your checkout page to allow your customers to subscribe to your newsletters.
